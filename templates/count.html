What are RTO procedures ?

Like many other RTO offices, Maharashtraâ€™s department has to look after registration, licenses and many other things. However, there are two main services that the Maharashtra RTO provides. One of them is vehicle registration, which ensures that the car belongs to its owner. Second is providing driver license. Every driver has to possess a license to drive a vehicle in the state of Maharashtra.

With those services, there are other things that Maharashtra RTO provides like pollution control, transfer certificate, temporary permit, special permit application, landing bill, registration renewal, temporary permit, all India tourist vehicle permit, goods carriage permit, and driving tests.
