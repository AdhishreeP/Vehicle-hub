Step-By-Step Process For Online Payment of National Permit

To have a national permit issued to your vehicle, you are required to pay a requisite amount as fee. Once you have applied for a national permit, a common question that arises later is how to pay national permit fee online. In this digital era, the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways has provided a simple solution to this question via its Parivahan Sewa portal.

To make your national permit online payment, you need to follow the steps mentioned below:

Step 1: Log on to the Parivahan Sewa portal.
Step 2: Under the ‘Online Services’ section on the menu bar, select ‘National Permit’.
Step 3: Under the ‘Payment and Transaction Status’ section, click on ‘Online Payment of National Permit’.
Step 4: You will then be redirected to a new page, where you will be prompted to enter your vehicle’s registration number and the last five characters of your chassis number.
Step 5: You can then make the composite fee payment via card or net banking.

The national permit authorisation for operating throughout the territory of India is granted to you after you have made the national permit payment.
