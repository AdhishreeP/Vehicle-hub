How to Check National Permit Status for your Vehicle

Once you have submitted your documents and made the payment to obtain your national permit, you may have to wait a few days before you get the national permit details for your vehicle. In order to perform a national permit online status check, you can simply follow the below steps:

Step 1: Log on to the Parivahan Sewa portal
Step 2: Under the ‘Online Services’ section on the menu bar, select ‘National Permit’
Step 3: On the new page where you will be redirected to, enter your vehicle’s registration number and the last five characters of your chassis number.
