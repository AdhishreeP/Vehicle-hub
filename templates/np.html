National permit issuance process

In India, on the basis of the type and utility of the vehicle, permits are issued that enable vehicles to move from one place to another. These permits are applicable for two kinds of vehicles: goods carrying vehicles and passenger carrying vehicles. Such vehicles need to apply for either a State Permit or a National Permit.
A National Permit, also known as an All India National Permit, is an official document that provides authorisation to goods carrying vehicles to move out of their home state. According to rule 86 and 87 of the Central Motor Vehicles Act, 1989, a national permit is issued for at least four states, in addition to the state where it was applied for. Learn all about the national permit system, how to apply for a national permit and all other relevant national permit details.
