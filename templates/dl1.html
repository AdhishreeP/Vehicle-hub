Types of Driving License in Maharashtra

You can opt for a driving license in Maharashtra on the basis of the type of vehicle you wish to drive. The state transport department of Maharashtra offers you the below types of driving license you wish to operate.

Transport Vehicle: Applicants who want to drive commercial and goods carriage vehicles can apply for this driving license type.

A non-transport driving license type is based on the following categories of DL:

• Motorcycles with gear: this category includes all motor vehicle types having gear transmission.
• Motorcycles without gear: this category includes vehicles such as mopeds and scooters
• Light motor vehicle: this category includes hatchbacks, sedans, etc.

Eligibility Criteria for obtaining a Driving License in Maharashtra

Before applying for a driving license, it’s important you meet the requirements of the eligibility criteria for obtaining a DL in Maharashtra.

• To be eligible to apply for a permanent driving license in Maharashtra, an individual should first have a learner’s license.
• An individual can apply for a permanent DL 30 days after the learner’s license is issued and within 180 days from the date of issue of the learner’s license
• To apply for a driving license for a two-wheeler with engine capacity not exceeding 50 cc, individuals should be above the age of 16 years.
• To apply for a driving license for a motor vehicle such as a car, sedan, etc, an individual should be above the age of 20 years.

Documents required for obtaining a Driving License in Maharashtra

The below documents are required for obtaining a driving license in Maharashtra.

• Age proof: (An applicant can submit any one document from the following)

1. Birth Certificate
2. Voter’s ID card
3. School leaving certificate
4. Pan card


5. Passport

6. Address proof: (An applicant can submit any one document from the following)

• Passport
• Life insurance policy
• Ration card
• Utility bills such as electricity bill, telephone bill, etc
• Pay slip issued by the state or central government

Other documents:

• Original learner’s license
• Application form no.4
• 3 passport size photographs
