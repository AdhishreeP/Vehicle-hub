Driving License in Maharashtra

A driving license is one of the most important documents you can possess as it gives you the legal freedom to drive your motorised vehicle on Indian roads. A DL also serves as a document of your identity proof, age and address proof. The State Transport Department of Maharashtra is responsible for the swift working of the road transport system in the state and one of its primary duties is to issue a driving license to its citizens.
